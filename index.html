World Cup Record

Brazil is the only nation to have played in every FIFA World Cup tournament.

Fastest Goal

The fastest goal in World Cup history was scored by Hakan Şükür of Turkey after just 11 seconds in 2002.

Oldest Player

Essam El-Hadary of Egypt is the oldest player to appear in a World Cup match at 45 years and 161 days.

Most Goals

Cristiano Ronaldo holds the record for most international goals with 123 goals for Portugal.
